Pentaho World 2017

Pentaho World will be held in Orlando, October 25 to 27. It has great speakers, topics and sponsors. You can get to know better Pentaho and what’s being done by and on Customers.

Real use cases with where companies are sharing their success, and who know you are there next year.

Go to meet the experts on one-to-one sessions of 45 minutes where you can get advice for your unique use case from the Pentaho experts. You can also take your laptop and get hands on training where you get to know more about specific topics

You will only have one problem, you might need to decide on your agenda. With the great agenda that was prepared the only problem will be that you wont be able to do so much as you would like.

Get a better idea by looking at the blog pots “10 Reasons to Attend Pentaho World 2017” that you can access using this link.
